About the Hosts

The Voices Behind
the Mic

MP
Manasvini Padmanabhan
Co-Host & Co-Founder

Manasvini brings a sharp analytical lens and genuine curiosity to every conversation. Passionate about business strategy and the stories that shape tomorrow's builders, she co-created Built Before 20 to give young entrepreneurs a space to learn and grow.

AH
Alina Hatia
Co-Host & Co-Founder

Alina is driven by the belief that big ideas don't wait for the right age. With a passion for business, culture, and the entrepreneurial mindset, she brings energy and vision to each episode — asking the questions that actually matter.

✦ Class of 2029

Manasvini and Alina are members of the class of 2029 who launched Built Before 20 in early 2026 with one goal: to create a space where young people could talk seriously about business, entrepreneurship, and what it means to build something meaningful before the world tells you that you're ready. Each episode covers business topics, entrepreneurial thinking, success stories, and the ideas shaping the next generation of founders.
